Founder of Vasuki NFC - Hem Shankar Agarwal

Know about Hem Shankar Agarwal, the Founder and Owner of Vasuki NFC, a Jaipur-based brand focused on smart NFC business cards and digital profile solutions.

Who is the Founder of Vasuki NFC?

Hem Shankar Agarwal is the Founder and Owner of Vasuki NFC. Vasuki NFC is a Jaipur-based brand that provides smart NFC business cards, QR digital visiting cards, Google profile cards, and digital business solutions for modern businesses and professionals.

The main idea behind Vasuki NFC is to replace old paper visiting cards with smart, digital, and easy-to-share business identity solutions. With NFC and QR technology, users can share their contact details, WhatsApp, call button, Instagram, website, location, and complete digital profile through one tap or scan.

About Vasuki NFC

Vasuki NFC is built to help shop owners, business owners, coaches, freelancers, institutes, service providers, and professionals create a strong digital presence. Instead of carrying multiple paper cards, a person can use one smart NFC or QR card to share all important business details instantly.

Vasuki NFC provides different types of smart card solutions, including basic QR cards, premium NFC cards, and metal NFC cards. These cards are useful for networking, customer connection, lead generation, and digital branding.

Vision Behind Vasuki NFC

The vision of Hem Shankar Agarwal behind Vasuki NFC is to make digital identity simple and affordable for every business. Many small businesses still depend only on paper visiting cards, which can be lost or thrown away. Vasuki NFC gives them a smarter way to connect with customers.

Through Vasuki NFC, businesses can create a professional digital profile that opens on any smartphone. This helps customers save contact details, visit social media pages, open maps, contact on WhatsApp, and view business information easily.

Why Vasuki NFC Was Started

Vasuki NFC was started to solve a simple problem: traditional visiting cards are limited, but businesses need a smarter and faster way to share details. A digital NFC card can be updated, shared, scanned, and used again and again without reprinting every time.

Hem Shankar Agarwal started Vasuki NFC with the aim of helping local businesses, professionals, and service providers move towards modern digital networking.
