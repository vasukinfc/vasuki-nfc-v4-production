Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how VASUKI NFC collects, uses, and protects customer information.

Information we collect

We may collect customer name, mobile number, email address, business name, delivery address, design details, uploaded logo/design files, and payment/order information.

How we use information

Customer information is used to process orders, create digital profiles, print cards, deliver products, provide customer support, and send order updates.

Payment information

Payments may be processed through third-party payment providers such as Razorpay. We do not store full card or bank details on our website.

Analytics and cookies

We may use tools like Google Analytics to understand website traffic, page views, and customer behavior. This helps us improve our website and services.

Data sharing

We do not sell customer data. Information may be shared only with required service providers such as payment gateways, courier partners, or technical service providers for order processing.

Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect customer information, but no online system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at vasukinfc@gmail.com.
